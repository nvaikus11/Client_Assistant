# REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — Excerpt (FICTIONAL SAMPLE)

> ⚠️ **Fictional sample.** "Aurora Grocery Group" is invented. No real client or
> Deloitte confidential information. For demo and testing use only.

**Issued by:** Aurora Grocery Group, Inc. (AGG) — Strategic Sourcing
**Title:** Enterprise Demand Forecasting & Data Modernization
**RFP Date:** 2026-02-10  **Responses due:** 2026-03-06

## 1. Business Context
AGG operates 480 stores across 28 states. Category managers forecast demand in
spreadsheets using trailing averages. Leadership has set two enterprise goals for
FY26–27: **reduce out-of-stocks** (currently ~6% on fresh categories) and **release
working capital** from excess and obsolete inventory (estimated ~$90M tied up).

## 2. Objectives
1. Unify demand-relevant data (POS, inventory, ERP, e-commerce) into one governed platform.
2. Replace spreadsheet forecasting with an ML capability, proven on pilot categories first.
3. Build internal skills to operate and extend the capability.

## 3. Required Capabilities
- Cloud lakehouse / modern data platform experience at retail scale.
- Production ML / MLOps (not just notebooks).
- Demonstrated grocery or retail supply-chain domain expertise.
- Change management and enablement, not just technology delivery.

## 4. Evaluation Criteria
| Criterion | Weight |
|-----------|-------:|
| Relevant retail/grocery experience | 25% |
| Technical approach & platform fit | 25% |
| Team quality & delivery model | 20% |
| Enablement / change management | 15% |
| Commercials | 15% |

## 5. Current Landscape (Incumbent)
- On-prem enterprise data warehouse (legacy, batch nightly, hard to extend).
- A BI reporting vendor for dashboards.
- Internal analytics team of 3; no production ML today.

## 6. Indicative Roadmap & Budget
- Vendor selection by **mid-March 2026**; mobilization in **April**.
- **Phase 1** (foundation + pilot) targeted to complete by **end of Q2 2026**.
- **Phase 2** (scale-out + advanced use cases) decision in **Q3 2026**.
- Indicative Phase 1 budget guidance: **$1.0M–$1.3M**.

## 7. Evaluation Notes (from AGG)
- The CFO's office requires a quantified ROI case before funding any scale-out.
- Strong preference for a partner who can transfer capability, not create dependency.
